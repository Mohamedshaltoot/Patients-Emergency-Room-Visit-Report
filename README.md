# 🏥 Patients Emergency Room Visit Analysis (Power BI)

## 📝 Project Overview
This professional Power BI dashboard provides a deep dive into **Emergency Room (ER) Patient Data** for the years 2019 and 2020. It is designed to help hospital administrators monitor patient flow, satisfaction levels, and wait times to optimize healthcare delivery.

## 🚀 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
* **👥 Total Patients Visit:** 9,216 (Split between 50.04% Administrative & 49.96% Non-Administrative).
* **⭐ Avg Satisfaction Score:** 5.47 / 10.
* **⏳ Avg Wait Time:** 35.26 Minutes.
* **📊 Referral Rate:** 58.59% Referred vs. 41.41% Walk-in patients.

## 🔍 Key Insights & Features
* **📅 Temporal Trends:** Analysis of patient volume by year (2019 vs 2020) and month-over-month trends (Peak in August with 1,024 visits).
* **🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Demographics:** Detailed breakdown by Age Group (Adults are the majority at 7,106) and Gender (51.1% Male).
* **🏥 Department Analysis:** Tracking referrals across General Practice, Orthopedics, Cardiology, and more.
* **🌡️ Heatmap Analysis:** A specialized matrix showing wait times and satisfaction scores across different patient races and age groups.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
* **Tool:** Power BI Desktop 📊
* **Data Cleaning:** Power Query (ETL Process) ⚙️
* **Calculations:** DAX (Advanced Measures & KPIs) 🔢
* **Visuals:** Heatmaps, Area Charts, and Custom Slicers 🎨
